History

In 1974 Fernando bought his first few tea estates to try his hand at producing Sri Lankan tea, and in 1981 he founded Ceylon Tea Services Limited.

George Steuart

About the Company

Sri Lanka's oldest mercantile establishment was The George Steuart of the Companies,established in 1835 during British occupation of Ceylon.

Having begun operations as a merchant banker and then a produce broker, now George Steuart have very own George Streuart Tea,exports,real estate,shipping.health and many more.




History

The Company traces its history to a British sea captain, Captain James Steuart(1790-1870), who sailed the southern seas in the early 1800s and settled in 1818 in Ceylon.Captain Steuart, seeing the commercial potential of the island, established himself as a merchant and a banker.Eventually, Steuart was appointed 'Master Attendant of the Roads of Colombo,' a role that prevented him from continuing his business activities.As a consequence, he welcomed his younger brother George (1808-1896) to take over the firm, which resulted in George Steuart and Company being founded in 1835.The Sri Lankan government initiated a nationalisation policy for privately owned tea plantations in 1975, leading to the purchase of 90% of the group's assets, the largest tea plantation acreage at the time.
